Promotion event


My senior project focuses on making a business plan to increase brand awareness for a new start-up clothing brand – Septup – through utilising the trendy tool of social media. The aim is to achieve the intended level of sales of 600 pieces in the timeframe of one month. The target customer segment of the age between 20 to 40 years old. Specifically, promotion of the brand clothing style based on comfortable business casual wear with a focused segment of women. This report will demonstrate the comprehension and application of academic knowledge acquired from the previous three years of study in combination with conveying the benefits and lessons learnt from undergoing this project. Lastly, how this project experience would assist and prepare me for my future career.



There are numerous ways to do online promotion for a clothing brand. However, due to the limited budget and the pandemic crisis we decided to do a live streaming event on the platform—Taobao – to promote the brand. The project demonstrated the application of the knowledge I learnt over the past three years as it assisted me to carry out the series of activities for successfully implementing the online event. An example of the application is designing the trailer posters for the live streaming event. In the process of designing, I utilised ranges of software such as PS, AI and the operation of this software is something I have learned from the courses I took before. Thus, the content learnt from the courses was very practical in helping me to prepare the fundamental parts of advertisement and marketing without the need to go and search for individuals who are expert in this field.

What is more of the comprehension of the content is the importance of selling point when marketing a product. The initial plan of the project tailoring the product to customers such as mother and daughters to be sold on Mother’s Day. Yet, the date chosen did not match with the date given by the manager of the live stream platform. Consequently, we switched and adapted to a new immediate plan of focusing on comfortable business wear for women at work. This illustrated the essentiality of the flexibility of the team and plan when encountering unpredictable circumstances. Hence, the appreciation to possess an agile plan for the designed product is essential for the ultimate success as projects can only be brought forward without fixated on the initial desired plan. Furthermore, the change in date meant that the live streaming event time posted on the preview poster on social media needs to be changed. This meant that I had to change it smoothly in a short period as well as explain to those people who have subscribed to watch the event. Moreover, in this project, I learned how to utilize hot news as promotion materials to help the niche brand improve its popularity and attractiveness. For example, in the initial promotion content, I only made a trailer poster to post on several the brand’s official social media accounts including Weibo and Xiaohongshu. However, at the time of that we need to reschedule time for the event and repost new version of promotion content to let audience to know this updating information, the news about Xinjiang cotton banning became a headline. I think it is good opportunity to use this headline as our promotion materials because some parts of clothing the brand were made by Xinjiang cotton and I also want to use this opportunity to show audience that we keep supporting Made in China as a Chinese clothing brand. As expected, the new version of promotion content posted on the brand’s official social media accounts got lots of attention. Many people leave many comments below it and they said that they look forward to the brand’s the first live streaming shopping event. Through undertaking the promotion and branding module in the previous years of study, I applied the knowledge from the course to better restructure and reorganize the language of the announcement released on social media.

When pursuing the project, I faced the uncontrollable challenge of internet problems during the live streaming event. The application of basic operation knowledge of equipment such as the light sets, placement of the camera, all was gained from the photography and shooting course I took at Drexel before. Even with these pieces of knowledge gained, I faced the challenge of internet instability which was a factor that I was unable to control. Due to the unstable Wi-Fi connection, the live streaming event automatically quit for a few minutes, which brought inconvenience to the audience and minor disruption to the entire event. Yet, I dealt with it calmly and successfully through delegating the roles, which demonstrated my strength of leadership. The assistant immediately contacted relevant backstage technicians and find out how to restore the live-streaming as soon as possible. My job was to explain the situation to the audience charmingly through the chat room in order to minimize the concerns and negativity. In addition, we clarified the clothing again in the live streaming to ensure that every product was well-explained and marketed to the intended audiences. Although, the first run through test went well and we had prepared thoroughly the probability of an issue appearing still exist. Therefore, in my future career, I should continue with my calm behaviour when encountered a problem in order to solve it effectively and efficiently.

As discussed previously, the change of the live stream date was a challenge for me because of the need to not only redesign and communicate the adjustment, but also the need to change the entire design theme due to this issue because it is no longer Mother's Day theme orientated. Subsequently, I faced the challenge of design in terms of communication with the factory collaborated. The alteration of design from material to decoration cost us a lot of time which caused delays. From this aspect, I reflected that in the future, I should confirm the main process before moving towards other steps as one change in the central stage would cause delay for the entire process. Therefore, the experience from this project was valuable for my future career as I recognise the importance of making sure the accuracy of each stage ensure efficiency. Besides, the first promotion text of the live stream event posted on social media had little effect to increase publicity and awareness of the stream. I dealt with it by changing the trailer using the knowledge learnt from the course which obtained favourable results. More people left messages and commented on the post after the change. Nevertheless, with all the challenges faced during the project and few operational glitches during the event, the result was uplifting. As a result of the live stream, more than 1,000 people watched the show, which was better than expected.



Technology plays a significant role in all our lives with live streaming becoming ever-trendier. Whether large or small enterprises are interested and will definitely utilise live streaming to promote their products and improve their services. Social media platforms are a unique medium for all to reach their target audience. The opportunity of undergoing this project focusing on marketing through live streaming is undoubtedly beneficial for my future career. From the perspective that, I learnt to function the popular medium which allowed video monetization in the special circumstance of the COVID-19 pandemic together with the cost-effective way when we are under tight financial budget.

The experience of planning and pursuing the live stream event equipped me with a new set of skills, which are very relevant for the preparation of my future career. As the contemporary world is becoming ever more internet-connected, with the majority of the population that are reliant on their mobile technology. The experience of the live stream and the detailed knowledge of working with different stakeholders such as designers and factory owners gave me the opportunity to enhance my interpersonal skills of communication and negotiation. I can better utilise the new function of social media and related medium to promote products for the company. Moreover, the live interactive experience tested my attitude and behaviour when faced with unpredicted issues, where I discovered my capability to adjust effectively in changing circumstances. Additionally, I gained knowledge from another field such as the textile industry. Where I expanded my horizon in terms of choosing fabrics, communicate the details with the factory owner which will be practical for preparing for my future career.

Despite the fact that we did not achieve the intended sales level of 600 pieces of clothing for the reason that the factory did not deliver several pieces of clothes on time. This meant that the models were not able to try them on to display them to the audiences on the day of the live streaming event. As a result, I reflected that the main reason for this issue resulted from the time wasted in the communication with the factory concerning the detailed improvement of clothes. Therefore, in the future career when preparing for anything, I need to make sure all the tiny processes are followed and ensured to prevent any barriers that may alter the intended result. Overall, pursuing this project was fruitful concluded from the aspect of act as a tool to reach a wider customer community and have equipped me with the knowledge and skills preparing me for my future career. Also, I think this project can be a case or working experience on my resume.
